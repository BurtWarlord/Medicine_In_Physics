Medicine in Physics
A University of Toronto Conference
Medicine in Physics is a conference held in Toronto, Ontario at the University of Toronto. Multiple professors and researchers from universities across Canada will come together to share their experiences and knowledge in linking aspects of the physics field to the ever-changing, complex field of medicine. The conference will be open to any individuals studying at a Canadian university within fields related to physics, engineering, or mechanics. The event will run from Friday September 10th 2021 to Monday September 13th 2021. Speak to members of your faculty at your university for more information on how to get signed up. Spots will be limited due to the event following COVID-19 safety precautions. Masks must be worn at all times while individuals are present on campus.
